## v1.4.2 — HOLD ghost preview

A tiny polish: when the HOLD slot is empty, the panel now shows a low-opacity ghost (18%) of the **current** piece instead of empty space. It hints at what hitting `C` will store, and gives the desktop HUD a calmer, more intentional look.

Implementation: reuses `drawCell({ ghost: true })` inside the HOLD mini-canvas — same code path as the on-board landing preview. No new rendering primitives.
